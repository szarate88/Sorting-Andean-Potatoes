Sorting Andean Potatoes Project

Welcome to the Interactive Tables

This project focuses on exploring the biodiversity of native Andean potatoes. In these tables, you will find information on the different descriptors used in recent regional potato catalogs from Huancavelica (2021) and La Libertad (2023). Explore a variety of descriptors including:

  • Morphological and agronomic descriptors like shape, color, yield, frost and late blight resistance.
  • Alternative names each variety has, including metaphorical and non-metaphorical names.
  • Cultural uses and gastronomic uses.
